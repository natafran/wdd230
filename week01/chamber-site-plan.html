Overview

Purpose

This site provides a place for individuals to learn more about the SCV Chamber and how we work to ensure our local businesses achieve economic prosperity through an engaged, innovative, and proactive business community. We encourage local business owners to take advantage of our ever-growing business network, the influence our organization has in the community, and our value-driven programming.

Scenarios

What is the main purpose of a chamber of commerce? What are the benefits of being a member of the chamber of commerce? How can I be added to the Chamber of Commerce annual business directory?
